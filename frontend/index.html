About KALONGO FARM

KALONGO FARM is an ecosystem farm lodge located in KIWIRA TUKUYU, Mbeya Region, Tanzania, combining sustainable agriculture with hospitality services. Founded on agro-ecology and environmental conservation principles, the facility offers accommodation in A-Cabins, Cottages, and Kikota structures, providing guests with farm-to-table dining experiences and immersive agricultural activities.
